Short & honest

We’re four instrumentalists with different musical roots — metal, rock and progressive, but also funk and jazz left their marks. That mix is exactly where our sound comes from: varied, rhythmic, sometimes intentionally unexpected — but always with a clear core.

Complexity isn’t the goal. If it gets intricate, it’s because it serves the song: tension, contrast, a moment that pushes the story forward. We love sharp edges — and we also love the short breaths of air before it slams back in.

What we stand for

Beyond the music, we care about a respectful space — at shows and in the community. If you’re in the room with us, you should feel safe. Energy over ego. Period.

Live

Live is where the songs really land: tight playing, pressure, tension — and then the moment where the door gets kicked in.

Guitar

Hi, I’m Jannik — I’ve been playing guitar for about 18 years. I recently moved to Aachen and I’m always up for ambitious band projects. Through many gigs and different lineups I’ve built a lot of live and project experience. Stylistically I’m into rock/progressive, but also soul, funk, blues and jazz. I love writing original songs and shaping arrangements. Influences include TOTO, Focus, Pink Floyd, The Night Flight Orchestra and more. What matters to me: motivated people, reliability, and the drive to build something real — no ego, just quality.
